About us

Virtual Workplace provides user ability to create his own office in Virtual Reality and extend his working environment in a way that was never available before.
One of our primary concerns is mobility. Traditional way to extend working environment is to attach additional monitors to your computer. But this approach is expensive, complex and it grately reduces your mobility.

We are giving user ability to interact with all his usual applications in a new way. We are using Virtual Reality headset to display the information. And providing support for usuall input devices like mouse and keyboard as well as new ways of interacting with environment like Intel RealSense.
